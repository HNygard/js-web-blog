Prince's first album of new material in two years follows on the heels of the recent Top Five success of "The Most Beautiful Girl In The World." Joining forces with The New Power Generation for a tightly focused set of jams, "Come" is ten new tracks from one of the most consistently brilliant artists in popular music.

Beginning with the jazzy 11-minute invocation of the title tune to the self-explanatory "Orgasm" at the end, the album features some of Prince's most audacious work ever, from the techno bounce of "Loose!" to the unflinching narrative of "Papa."

"Solo" was co-written with Tony Award-winning playwright David Henry Hwang (M. Butterfly).

Videos have already been shot for several songs from the album, and single choices are forthcoming.

This album will take a parental advisory logo.
