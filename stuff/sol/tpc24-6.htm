After thrilling the dust-covered hordes on Lollapalooza '93, moving over 300,000 copies of "Where You Been" and emerging as an antihero for a generation, J Mascis's moment of ultimate glory has arrived. The new Dinosaur Jr album is a thing of unsurpassed beauty and volume designed to make that band's frontman/main_man/Renaissance_man a star evermore.

As usual, J played just about everything on the album himself (except bass, courtesy of Mike Johnson). Thalia Zedek of Come contributes backing vocals on three tracks.

"Feel The Pain" is the album's first single and video, a brilliant slab of angst 'n' roll performed in the mighty Mascis manner.

Count on an all-out promo/publicity barrage to make this album unspeakably huge.

Dinosaur Jr will tour until every human being in North America suffers from partial hearing loss.
